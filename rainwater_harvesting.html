Effects of Drought

Drought is a prolonged period of insufficient rainfall that causes water shortages. It has severe consequences for agriculture, the environment, and human life.

Major Effects of Drought:

  • Crop failure and food shortages
  • Water scarcity for drinking and irrigation
  • Loss of livestock and wildlife
  • Increased risk of wildfires
  • Soil degradation and desertification
  • Economic losses and poverty
  • Health issues due to malnutrition and poor sanitation
